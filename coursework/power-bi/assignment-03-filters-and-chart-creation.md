# Assignment 03: Filters and Chart Creation

This assignment focused on building Power BI visuals for sales, quantity, categories, sub-categories, products, customers, and time-based trends.

## Visuals Created

| Screenshot | Visualization | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `Assignment 03 Question 01.png` | Stacked column chart: Sum of Sales by Year and Sub-Category | Compares yearly sales across selected sub-categories such as Accessories, Chairs, and Copiers. |
| `Assignment 03 Question 03.png` | Bar chart: Sum of Quantity by Customer Name | Shows top customers by total quantity ordered. |
| `Assignment 03 Question 05.png` | Bar chart: Count of Order ID by Sub-Category | Compares order counts across sub-categories such as Furnishings, Chairs, Tables, and Bookcases. |
| `Assignment 03 Question 07.png` | Bar chart: Sum of Sales by Category with date slicer | Compares sales by category while allowing filtering by order date. |
| `Assignment 03 Question 09.png` | Line chart: Sum of Sales by Month and Sub-Category with slicer buttons | Shows monthly sales trends across many sub-categories. |
| `Assignment 03 Question 11.png` | Line chart: Sum of Sales by Year and Quarter | Shows sales trends over time using a year and quarter hierarchy. |
| `Assignment 03 Question 13.png` | Horizontal bar chart: Sum of Sales by Category, Sub-Category, and Product Name | Highlights product-level sales within category and sub-category groupings. |

## Skills Practiced

- Creating bar, column, and line charts
- Applying filters and slicers
- Using date ranges
- Building hierarchy-based visuals
- Comparing categories and sub-categories
- Reading measures such as Sum of Sales, Sum of Quantity, and Count of Order ID
- Formatting report pages for readability
